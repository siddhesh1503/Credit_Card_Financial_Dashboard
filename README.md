# 💳 Credit Card Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Credit Card Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution developed to analyze customer demographics, spending behavior, transaction trends, revenue generation, and interest earnings. The dashboard helps financial institutions gain actionable insights into customer segments and card performance, enabling data-driven business decisions.

The project consists of two reports:

1. **Credit Card Customer Report**
2. **Credit Card Transaction Report**

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and behavior.
- Monitor revenue and interest earnings.
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Track transaction performance across quarters.
- Understand customer spending patterns.
- Compare performance across card categories.
- Support strategic business decisions.

## Customer Report Key Findings

- Businessmen generate the highest revenue and interest earnings.
- Customers aged 40–50 contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Graduate customers form the largest revenue-generating educational segment.
- California, Texas, and New York are among the highest revenue-contributing states.
- High-income customers contribute a major share of overall revenue.
